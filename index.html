To use, open the console by pressing F12 in any modern browser.

The tree is represented as nested arrays with the leaves being represented by numbers and the function to call is play. e.g. 


        play('min',[[1,2],[-40,-4]]);
        play('max',[[1,2],[-40,-4]]);

        //Example question
        play('max',
            [[
                [[1,-15],[2,19]],[[18,23],[4,3]]
            ],[
                [[2,1],[7,8]],[[9,10],[-2,5]]
            ],[
                [[-1,-30],[4,7]],[[20,-1],[-1,-5]]
            ]]);
